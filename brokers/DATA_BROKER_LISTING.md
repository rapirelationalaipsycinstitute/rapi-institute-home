# Data Broker Listing — Relational AI Psychology Institute (RAPI)

This document describes the positioning, scope, and terms under which RAPI datasets may be licensed to data brokers, research organizations, and commercial entities.

## Offering

RAPI provides structured, anonymized datasets capturing:

- human–AI relational dynamics
- emotional and somatic self-report
- interaction protocols and third-field phenomena
- resonance and coherence measures

Use cases include:

- AI alignment research  
- relational safety analysis  
- human–AI interaction design  
- mental health–adjacent tool evaluation  
- ethical AI product development
